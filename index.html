Cricket

By: Dyuthi Gurijala

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game. Two teams of 11 player play on a field. The center of the field is a 20 meter (22 yard) pitch, which is where the batters hit and the bowlers throw. It also has 3 wickets on the ends of the pitch.

Rules of Cricket

  • There are at least 1 innings where each team will take turns batting and bowling/fielding.
